## Full description

### English
Stop scrolling lists. HaMakom gives you **one strong answer** to "where should we go?"

Answer five quick questions — city, vibe, how serious it is, how long you want the
evening to be — and get a complete, realistic date plan: where to meet, what to
order, where to walk, and where to end the night. With maps, timing, and budget.

Built for observant Jewish singles in Israel:
💎 Curated places, including hidden gems locals actually go to
🍷 Wineries, lookouts, cafés, museums and experiences across the country
🕯 Knows the week — Thursday-night plans, Motzei-Shabbat picks
💬 Share the plan on WhatsApp in one tap, or send an invite
❤️ Save plans and places; they sync across your devices when you sign in
🇮🇱 Fully bilingual — Hebrew and English, RTL done right

No swiping, no profiles, no noise. Just the plan.
